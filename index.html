About Us

We are certified and insured pool inspectors located in Melbourne. Our purpose
is to provide you with essential information for your inspection, assisting you in obtaining swift and hassle-free Certificate of Barrier Compliance.

  • Free Quote & Phone Consultation
  • Inspector Registration Number: IN-PS 100009
  • Service across Melbourne's east and Regional areas

It is mandatory for owners of land where a swimming pool or spa is located to have their safety barriers inspected and to lodge a certificate of barrier compliance with their council.

For pools and spas constructed before 1 November 2020, a compliance certificate must be lodged by 1 June 2024.
